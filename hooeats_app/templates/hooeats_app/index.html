Hoo Eats

Plan your meals at UVA

Features

Search for Recipes

With over 1000 recipes, you will be sure to find something you enjoy. We have recipes for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks. Each recipe has ingredients, steps, nutritional information.

Explore Dining Hall Menus

You can explore weekly menus for all three dining halls at UVA - Runk, O'Hill, and Newcomb. Each menu item has nutritional information such as calories, total fat, carbohydrates, protein, and sugar. Add the dining hall meals you want to your weekly meal plan.

Make a Meal Plan

Plan out your meals for each week using our meal planner. Each weekly meal plan includes sections for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks. You can bookmark recipes and UVA dining hall meals that you like, and add them to your plan for the week.
